About Water Bodies in Delhi

Major Water Bodies in Delhi:

  • North Zone: DTU Lake, Bhalaswa Lake, and Johad - These water bodies play a crucial role in groundwater recharge and local ecosystem.
  • South Zone: Hauz Khas Lake, Tughlakabad Lake, and Hari Nagar Lake - Historical water bodies that serve both recreational and ecological purposes.
  • East Zone: Sanjay Lake, Vinod Nagar Lake, and NH-24 I.P Extension Lake - Important water bodies supporting local biodiversity.
  • West Zone: Sardar Patel Lake, Madipur Lake, and Nangloi Sayyed Lake - Vital water resources for western Delhi.
  • Central Zone: Naini Lake, India Gate Lake, and Rajghat Lake - Iconic water bodies in the heart of Delhi.
